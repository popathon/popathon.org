Popathon #BDF14

12-13 September 2014

Lighthouse Arts, Brighton

Hacking Stories at Brighton Digital Festival


This first Popathon in Brighton was a special edition in partnership with Lighthouse Arts, as part of the Brighton Digital Festival. People had travelled from far and the mix of participants was great. Three teams that included filmmakers, poets, creative technologists and interactive theatre makers, collaborated intensely and each created a web-native storytelling projects in under 24 hours!
