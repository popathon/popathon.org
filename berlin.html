Popathon Berlin

A series of Hack Jams exploring the future of Storytelling

Storytelling in the age of the web

We invite you to push the boundaries of storytelling by embracing the capability of the web. For 24 hours we're getting together with filmmakers, journalists, technologists and designers to brainstorm ideas, create open-source projects and demo prototypes of your own web-native stories.

Curious what's possible in 24 hours? Explore projects hacked at previous editions .

Why?

Storytelling has been with us since the beginning of time. Through the ages the way we tell stories has changed along with the evolution of the media we use to express ourselves.

The capability of the web enables stories to be interactive, dynamic and participatory, allowing for engaging experiences around them.

While this is hugely exciting, we believe a language of web-native storytelling is still developing and want to help that process along by hands-on experimentation.

How?

Teams compete against the clock, not against each other. By sharing ideas at an early stage, teams help each other to tackle challenges in the creative process.

We'll kick things off with a series of lightning talks to provoke and inspire. Throughout the hackathon, experienced mentors will lend their support to the creative process of the different teams. We encourage bringing ideas and content for projects you have in mind or in production.

After the final sprint all prototypes are presented in a live demo and all the code powering the projects will be made available open source for others to study and build on beyond the event.

Storytellers unite!

Who should join us at this event?

Teams thrive when different skills are combined.

We believe disciplines like journalism, filmmaking, web development and design already offer the most important skills required to tell engaging stories on the web.

By sharing diverse expertises and perspectives around the table, we are able to realize web-native storytelling together and:

  • Meet, exchange & collaborate with multifarious professionals
  • Brainstorm and prototype ideas for a new medium
  • Create visually-rich interactive multimedia projects

Digital Storytelling

You are a filmmaker, journalist, radio-maker, researcher, writer, photographer or communications professional with a story to tell? Explore how your story can become dynamic and interactive!

Web / App Development

You are a developer, creative coder or computer scientist hungry for creative exploration? Experiment with new libraries and frameworks in an informal setting!

Digital Design

Are you a designer or artist with skills from UX, IxD, graphic, video or service design interested in all things digital? Co-create meaningful experiences, combining moving image and interactivity and help bringing web-native stories to live!
